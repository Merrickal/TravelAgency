About the Hotel

Lti Bellevue Park Sousse


The Lti Bellevue Park Hotel is a magnificent multi-story resort. Spanning several square kilometers, it boasts modern architecture and an elegant décor with a traditional touch. This 5-star hotel provides high-quality services aimed at satisfying its guests, who find peace and serenity there. Lti Bellevue Park is simply a dream destination for a truly wonderful vacation.

of the Lti Bellevue Park Hotel Sousse Lti Bellevue Park emerges beautifully facing the fine sandy beach, surrounded by immense palm gardens. It is located almost 3 km from Port el Kantaoui. It is also very close to several tourist and sports sites. Monastir Airport is less than 20 minutes away.

Accommodation at the Lti Bellevue Park Hotel Sousse The Lti Bellevue Park Hotel has 387 luxurious rooms (double, triple, family, etc.), all equipped with a pleasantly furnished balcony with a sea or garden view. All rooms have the amenities you would expect in a 5-star hotel. Indeed, they are all ultra-comfortable with a refined and welcoming décor and a modern design. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom with a bathtub/shower and various accessories, a television with satellite channels, a telephone, a safe, and a minibar. Air conditioning and Wi-Fi are available in all rooms. Friendly room service is available to guests at any time of the day and evening.

Restaurants & Bars at the Lti Bellevue Park Hotel Sousse The Lti Bellevue Park Hotel has two large restaurants: a main buffet restaurant and an à la carte themed restaurant. Both restaurants offer gourmet dishes of local and international cuisine. The property has 3 magnificent bars: a Lounge Bar where the atmosphere is always warm, a Pool Bar by the pool serving refreshing drinks and cocktails, and a main bar inside the hotel.

Activities and Leisure at the Lti Bellevue Park Hotel Sousse As its name suggests, the hotel has a large park where purity and freshness abound. In addition to the indoor pool with a jacuzzi, there is a large outdoor pool surrounded by sun loungers and parasols to fully enjoy the sun and read a magazine. The hotel has two tennis courts, a multi-sports ground, a 4-lane English bowling alley, and a large playground. For children, a well-equipped playground and a mini club are offered. The fitness center is equipped with a hammam, sauna, and massage centers, offering an extensive list of treatments.

Key features include: direct beach access, pools, activities, dining
