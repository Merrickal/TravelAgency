About the Hotel

El Mouradi Palace


Situation: Located on a superb beach, the El Mouradi Palace hotel is situated in the heart of the Port El Kantaoui tourist area, facing a 36-hole golf course and 10 km from the city of Sousse. The hotel is 35 km from Monastir-Habib Bourguiba Airport, 70 km from Yasmine Hammamet, and 140 km from Tunis-Carthage Airport.

Accommodation: The hotel has 311 rooms, including 10 connecting rooms, 2 apartments, 1 presidential suite, and 1 junior suite, served by 2 elevators, a full bathroom, with balcony and terrace, direct telephone, air conditioning, satellite TV, radio (in TV), mini bar, safe at the reception.

Restaurants and Bars: A restaurant for breakfast and dinner with 620 seats. An à la carte restaurant with 70 seats. A coffee shop with 150 seats. A barbecue/beach club. A lounge bar with 320 seats. A piano bar with 120 seats. A Moorish café with 70 seats. A pool bar with 500 seats. A nightclub with 250 seats.

Sports and Leisure: Table tennis, 5 tennis courts (lighting at extra charge), billiards, volleyball court, beach volleyball, ping pong.

Thalassotherapy and Well-being: Opened in 2006, the thalassotherapy center integrated into the hotel is perfectly equipped for quality treatments. With a capacity of 120 spa guests per day, the center has 30 treatment rooms, a beauty treatment room, a seawater pool, as well as a hammam and 2 saunas.

For children: Mini club, playgrounds, babysitting on request.

Credit cards accepted: Visa, Eurocard, Master Card, Amex, Diners Club. Pets are not allowed.

Key features include: large pools, conference center, thalassotherapy, restaurants
