Jaz Tour Khalef

About the Hotel

Discover the Jaz Tour Khalef Hotel: If you want to benefit from VIP treatment, then look no further than the Jaz Tour Khalef. It is located in a 10-hectare park. Enjoy a well-equipped private beach for a memorable stay.

Location of the Jaz Tour Khalef Hotel: Located in Sousse, 4 km from the Sousse Archaeological Museum and close to Port El Kantaoui. The hotel is 39.9 km from Enfidha-Hammamet Airport and a few minutes from tourist resorts.

Accommodation at the Jaz Tour Khalef Hotel: The property offers 262 rooms; standard, single with view, family rooms, and luxurious suites. All room categories are equipped with air conditioning, a bathroom, a flat-screen TV with satellite, a courtesy tray with daily replenishment of a bottle of mineral water, a mini-bar, and some with a balcony or terrace.

Services at the Jaz Tour Khalef Hotel: Restaurant & Bars: The hotel has 4 restaurants, including two à la carte restaurants with a wide choice of international and Tunisian cuisine, and another by the pool. It also has 6 bars offering a pleasant atmosphere and 4-star service.

Pool and Spa: The hotel has 4 outdoor pools, including 3 freshwater pools with slides (2 for children) and a relaxing seawater pool. The hotel has a 4,700 m² thalassotherapy and spa center. It includes cabins for massages, hydrotherapy, and beauty treatments, as well as a seawater pool. The property enjoys a privileged location with a direct and extensive view of the sea. Couples particularly appreciate the location of this property.

Activities and Leisure: The Jaz Tour Khalef offers a mini-club and a playground for children aged 4 to 12. You can enjoy snorkeling, water skiing, tennis, and golf courts. There is also a casino and a nightclub.

Free Services: Free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, as well as parking, air conditioning, heating, entertainment for children, etc.

Key features include: Thalasso center, pools, beachfront location
