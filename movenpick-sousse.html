About the Hotel

Mövenpick Resort & Marine SPA


Discover the Mövenpick Resort & Marine SPA: A high-end, 5-star resort with access to a private beach, located along the waterfront of Tunisia's most vibrant city. A blend of urban and natural elements, a true haven of relaxation, the Mövenpick Resort & Marine Spa Sousse offers its guests a memorable stay. Location of the Mövenpick Resort & Marine SPA: Located in the Pearl of the Sahel, Sousse, the most sought-after and lively city on Tunisia's east coast. It is 17 km from Monastir Habib-Bourguiba Airport and 3 km from the bustling Medina of Sousse. The Mövenpick Resort & Marine Spa is a prestigious five-star beachfront hotel, surrounded by the city's finest nightclubs and 10 minutes from the Port El Kantaoui golf course.

Accommodation at the Mövenpick Resort & Marine SPA Sousse: The Mövenpick Resort & Marine Spa offers its guests 618 luxurious rooms and suites with the amenities that only a five-star hotel can provide, with a choice of smoking or non-smoking rooms and private balconies or terraces with magnificent views of the pool or the sea and direct access to the fitness center and outdoor pool. The various room types available have free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, a safe, a mini-bar, a tea and coffee maker... And some categories have balconies with a gulf view.

Services at the Mövenpick Resort & Marine SPA Sousse: Restaurants & Bars: The Mövenpick Resort & Marine Spa Sousse has 5 restaurants and 6 bars: La Villa: French restaurant with Mediterranean gastronomy, a unique concept combining a restaurant and several bars, each with a different ambiance. Le Tapeo Restaurant: À la carte restaurant and bar with Spanish cuisine that will delight the taste buds of all its customers in a Brussels-inspired atmosphere and a blend of indoor-outdoor spaces. Le Mosaïque Restaurant: An international cuisine buffet with a variety of dishes and show cooking. Le Sendai Restaurant: Offering fresh sushi in a picturesque setting and authentic teppan (Japanese cuisine on a hot plate) for a journey to the heart of Japan. Take advantage of our "Dinner Around" offer: allows guests the possibility to dine in one of its following à la carte restaurants while keeping the same half-board supplement: Le Tapeo restaurant or Le Sendai restaurant. Guests can enjoy a three-course meal (appetizer, main course, dessert) each day. Drinks are extra. It is necessary to reserve your place and ensure availability by calling 8606 after "Check-in" (Every day from 9 am to 5 pm).

Pools and Spa: The Mövenpick Resort & Marine Spa Sousse offers free access to its indoor pool and a large saltwater pool, as well as access to its well-equipped and maintained golden sandy beach. The Mövenpick Resort with its Marine & Spa center also provides a sauna and a wellness center.

Activities and Leisure: The Mövenpick Resort & Marine Spa Sousse dedicates a daily program, several workshops, and once a week, a short night-time outing to children aged 0 to 12, as well as a pool, an equipped playground, menus, shows, and competitions, everything needed to satisfy them. Several activities are available including aerobics, a large fitness room, beach volleyball, water aerobics, and weekly Japanese cooking classes. Evening Cultural and Elegant Entertainment: The Mövenpick Resort & Marine Spa Sousse ensures the entertainment of its guests in the evening with a program of live music evenings with a resident DJ. At the end of the night, guests can refresh themselves with a selection of international drinks and snacks at La Villa with daily live musical entertainment.

Free Services: The property offers free access to the pools, free Wi-Fi, free parking, facilities for the disabled, etc.

Key features include: spa, pools, restaurants, kids club
